# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Positive behaviors include:** empathy, respect, constructive feedback, and focusing on community benefit.

**Unacceptable behaviors include:** harassment, trolling, personal attacks, and publishing private information.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to conduct@hazelcast.com. All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly.
